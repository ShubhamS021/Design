About

This is a lifestyle-driven product, a mix between traditional single-family housing and multifamily apartments.

All the units are for rent and under one ownership ensuring the grounds are well maintained and building aesthetics are upheld to the highest standards.

Differing from most multifamily projects, this product is all single level units with a private back yard, placed in configurations of standalone and duplex units, which creates a much more single-family neighborhood feel.

Enjoy your living

The primary amenity area is centrally located with delineated pedestrian connections making it safe and efficient for residents to walk to its location

The primary amenity area features a 4,500 square foot clubhouse building that houses a fitness center with yoga studio, a large gathering lounge, a game room with kitchen facilities, spacious covered patios with seating, and restrooms. The leasing office also utilizes this building. Behind the clubhouse is the large community swimming pool with lap lanes and benches, a water feature, and a jacuzzi spa. Surrounding the pool is large sunning deck and large Ramada with fire pit.
