The parties to the Apartment Lease hereby enter into the following Security Deposit Addendum (hereinafter "Addendum"). Where provisions of this Addendum conflict with those set forth in the Apartment Lease, the provisions set forth herein shall supersede and be controlling.

Resident and Management have agreed that Resident has the obligation to provide Management with a security deposit at the commencement of the term of the Lease. Resident understands that security deposits, in addition to guarantying full and faithful performance of the lease by the Resident, protect Management and the owner of the community from property damage or other losses.

The opportunity to purchase security deposit insurance and renter's insurance from Management's preferred provider partner is available to all residents as an option to lower Resident's up-front move in costs. Resident is not required to purchase security deposit insurance and, if Resident chooses to not purchase both the security deposit insurance and renter's insurance from ePremium Insurance LLC, Resident agrees to deliver the security deposit to Management at the commencement of the term of the Apartment Lease in certified funds.

Resident has been presented two options regarding the security deposit: payment of the traditional security deposit or acquisition of a policy of Security Deposit Insurance through ePremium Insurance LLC, described below.

Resident hereby elects the following option with regard to the Security Deposit:

A. Traditional Security Deposit. Resident shall deliver to Management funds equal to the amount identified on the line "Security Deposit" in the third column ("Refundable Deposits") under the Monthly Rent, Non-Refundable Fees, and Refundable Deposits section of the Apartment Lease. Resident is aware that Resident must still obtain and maintain a policy of renter's insurance.

B. ePremium Security Deposit Insurance. Resident has purchased a policy of security deposit insurance and a policy of renter's insurance from ePremium Insurance LLC. Resident understands and agrees that maintaining these policies of insurance (and the payment of all monthly premiums in a timely manner) is a material obligation under the Apartment Lease.
